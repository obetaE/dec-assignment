Our Story

A Little Corner-Shop with Big Dreams

Founded with passion and a love for storytelling, Chalk began as a humble corner-shop dedicated to bringing the finest comic books to our community. Today, we've grown into a beloved destination for women who appreciate art, narrative, and quality.

What sets us apart is our commitment to handcrafted excellence. Each comic in our collection is carefully curated for its artistic merit, compelling characters, and stories that resonate. We believe in comics that feel personal, authentic, and alive.
